Why We Built OpalOps

I spent years as a stagehand and event tech worker. I saw the same problem over and over: companies hiring crew through gig platforms where workers have zero accountability, and businesses have zero compliance protection.

Most staffing platforms use 1099 contractors. That means compliance risk for you, instability for crews, and last-minute cancellations that ruin events.

"We built OpalOps the way I wish it existed when I was on the floor: W-2 employees who show up, full employment accountability, and no legal gray area for you."

That's it. OpalOps is what happens when a floor-level worker decides to solve the problem from the top.
