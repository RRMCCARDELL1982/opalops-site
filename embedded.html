How it works

Invisible by design.
Five steps to seamless.

From first contact to post-job wrap, your end-client never knows we exist. That's the point.

01 — Scope

Tell us about your program

You reach out with crew size, event type, and brand standards. We align on the details before anything is confirmed.

02 — Brief

We prep your crew

Before every job, crew receive a client-specific brief — your talking points, site protocols, and brand standards. They arrive knowing the role.

03 — Deploy

Crew shows up as your team

No OpalOps branding on the floor. Crew introduce themselves as part of your operation. Your end-client sees a unified, professional team.

04 — Execute

We manage everything behind the scenes

Timekeeping, compliance, payroll, and workers' comp — all handled on our end. You stay focused on your client.

05 — Invoice

One clean invoice to you

All paperwork and billing stays strictly between OpalOps and your company. Your end-client never sees a vendor name.

Single Invoice, Total Discretion
One clean invoice to your company. No OpalOps paperwork reaches your end-client. Billing structure stays entirely between us.
Pre-Job Client Brief
Before every deployment, your crew receives a written brief with your client's brand standards, talking points, site layout, and any protocols specific to the job.
