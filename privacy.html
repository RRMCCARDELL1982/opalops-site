Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 10, 2026

OpalOps (a DBA of RRM Services LLC) ("we," "us," or "our") operates opalops.us and related services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, including:

  • Name, email address, phone number, and company name
  • Project and event details submitted through our quote request form
  • Payment information processed securely through Stripe

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Prepare and deliver service quotes
  • Process payments and send invoices
  • Communicate about your project and staffing needs
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Payment Processing

All payments are processed by Stripe. OpalOps does not store credit card numbers or sensitive payment data on our servers. Stripe's privacy policy is available at stripe.com/privacy.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service providers necessary to operate our business (Stripe, Airtable)
  • Legal authorities if required by law

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide services and comply with legal obligations.

6. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us at info@opalops.us.
