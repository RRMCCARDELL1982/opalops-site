1. Services

OpalOps provides on-demand labor staffing services including event crews, stagehands, warehouse workers, general laborers, movers, and cleanup crews in the New York and New Jersey market. All crew members are full W-2 employees with workers' compensation, general liability insurance, and fidelity bond coverage.

2. Quotes and Pricing

All quotes are flat-rate and reflect the agreed scope of work based on the information provided in your crew request. Pricing is subject to change if crew requirements, hours, or job conditions change materially from the original request. Scope changes must be agreed upon in writing before the scheduled event date. Quote prices are valid for 14 days from issuance.

3. Payment Terms

Deposit & Balance

A 50% deposit is required to confirm crew booking and reserve your event date. The remaining 50% balance is due within 5 business days of job completion. For extended or multi-week engagements, clients will be invoiced weekly on Fridays, with payment due within 5 business days of invoice receipt.

Payment Methods

Payments are processed securely via Stripe. Accepted payment methods include: ACH (direct debit), credit or debit card, and business check. For check payments, funds must be received and cleared in advance of the scheduled start date to allow sufficient processing time.

Invoice Details

A 50% deposit invoice will be issued upon quote approval. Upon job completion, a final invoice will be issued for the remaining 50% balance. Invoices include itemized crew breakdown, hours, rates, and terms. Undisputed invoices become final after 24 hours of shift completion.

4. Late Payment & Fees

Invoices unpaid after 10 business days from issuance are subject to a late fee of 1.5% per month on the outstanding balance. Repeated late payments or non-payment may result in suspension of future services and referral to collections.

5. Cancellation Policy

Cancellation terms are tiered based on notice provided:

  • More than 48 hours before deployment: No charge. Full deposit refunded within 5 business days.
  • 24–48 hours before deployment: 50% of estimated labor cost charged. Remaining deposit refunded within 5 business days.
  • Less than 24 hours before deployment: 100% of estimated labor cost charged. No refund issued.
  • Same-day cancellation after crew dispatch: 100% of actual labor cost incurred (crew wages, mileage, equipment) charged immediately.

Cancellations must be communicated in writing via email to info@opalops.us. Cancellation time is measured from when OpalOps receives the cancellation notice, not when it was sent.

6. Timekeeping & Hour Disputes

OpalOps maintains official time records for all crew members deployed. Clients are responsible for reviewing and approving hours within 24 hours of shift completion. Time sheets are submitted via our crew management system or email. Undisputed hours become final after 24 hours and are not subject to revision. Any disputes must be raised in writing within 24 hours of shift completion.

7. Non-Solicitation

Clients may not directly solicit, hire, or engage any OpalOps worker introduced through our services for a period of 12 months following the last placement without written consent and payment of a conversion fee. The conversion fee is 20% of the worker's anticipated first-year compensation at the time of hire. This includes any direct hiring, contract work, or third-party placement. Violation of this clause may result in legal action for breach of contract.
