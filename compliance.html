Recent DOL Action on Misclassification

The U.S. Department of Labor has dramatically increased enforcement against companies misclassifying workers as 1099 contractors. If audited, violations can result in:

  • Back wages — Significant financial penalties reaching back multiple years
  • Fines — Up to $10,000+ per violation, multiplied across violations
  • Class action lawsuits — Especially in NY and CA, where state labor boards are aggressive
  • Public enforcement actions — Reputational damage and lost business

With OpalOps, you eliminate this risk entirely.
